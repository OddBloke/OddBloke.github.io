Stardom Elo Ratings

These are Elo Ratings for Stardom year-by-year, and also for all time. In case your browser is struggling to render any graph, there are also versions that are filtered to only include ratings for the top 50 from that time period (judged by their ratings at the end of the period). These pages are generated using the script(s) at https://gist.github.com/OddBloke/909f26da0d6b59b1339ac561b5dc08b9
